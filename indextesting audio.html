Tangierino Cuisine

The cuisine at Tangierino is a fusion of old and new world cooking, artfully ​blending North African spices and techniques with contemporary French, ​American, and Mediterranean style. The resulting dishes are masterfully ​assembled with vibrant and distinctive flavors, unique to Tangierino, but ​designed to appeal to a wide range of palates.

Click here to view our diverse menu selection.

 


Samad Naamad

Samad Naamad is a visual and culinary artist from which Tangierino ​restaurant, Koullshi Lounge and SNE entertainment were born. Tangierino ​stands as a symbol of his artistic passions and amalgamation of culinary and ​visual mediums. swirling together expertise in culinary arts, visual design, ​performance and film production. Samad Naamad is a quintessential creator, ​going to the ends of earth, and often sacrificing convention, in order to realize a ​vision of providing an entirely unique experience.

To learn more about Samad Naamad, click here to visit his website.

 


Koullshi

Voted “Boston’s Hottest Night Spot,” Koullshi is without question, a one-of-a-​kind exotic lounge where the most elegant, floral and fruity hookah and over 70 ​premium cigars are enjoyed in a vibrant, 5,000 square foot lounge created by ​excavating deep into the earth. A state-of-the-art air exchange system keeps ​the air clean and fresh for relaxing and enjoying 7 flat screens where something ​is always on and Koullshi offers a full bar and both private and intimate spaces ​and a separate cigar lounge to cater to a wide range of pleasures.

Belly Dancing

A nightly event at Tangierino and Koullshi, belly dancing is a mesmerizing and seductive art form with deep roots in Moroccan culture and Middle Eastern lore. The Belly Dance is a spiritual connection between mind and body and at Tangierino, it completes the total sensory experience, unleashes freedom, and most importantly, the ancient performance celebrates an inner spirit through expressive movement. Belly Dancing performances occur at Tangierino weekdays at 7:00PM and weekends at 7:30 and 9:30PM.

one of the most highly acclaimed and talked about culinary experiences in greater Boston, rich with visual elegance, exotic tastes and masterful service. Come savor what has helped Tangierino earn unique distinction, year after year, as “City’s Best,” “Most Romantic,” “Most Exotic,” and “A Boston Top 10.”

 

Tangierino is a one-of-a-kind and exceptionally eclectic restaurant that combines the deeply-developed Moroccan culinary culture with a contemporary infusion of French, Mediterranean and American Chophouse influences. The resulting cuisine is out-of-this-world unique, deliciously decadent and appeals to a very wide range of tastes.

From the magic flavors induced by clay tagines, to a charbroiled rack of lamb dusted with exotic spices to the sesame-kissed tuna loin, to our rich, fork-tender braised short rib with fig, the unmistakable flavors of Tangierino are alluring and memorable. Experience the sensual, rose water handwashing ritual and dine surrounded by inspiring décor underneath flowing tapestries on ornate tables dappled with fresh rose petals. Tangierino is a one-of-a-kind place to be savored.

  •              Lavishly appointed with rich tapestries, heavy drapes, bejeweled chandeliers and hammered brass, Tangierino oozes both opulence and decadence. It’s not just romantic, it’s downright sensual.

Tangierino’s creative adaptations on both new and old-world cuisines represent some of the most sumptuous fusions around, delicately lacing dishes with cinnamon, sticky figs, fresh lemon, smoky eggplant, garlic and complex olives. Traditional clay tagines impart wonderful roasted subtleties and tenderness to classic North African dishes while our intensely hot grill sears and flavors prime steaks and chops which are expertly prepared and artfully plated by our experienced chef.
