 Private Functions

 

Tangierino is widely regarded as a unique venue for celebrating special events and business dinners, catering to private events of 10 to 300 people. To inquire about holding your private function at Tangierino, call 617.242.6009 or use this form to coordinate and customize your needs and share your ideas. Tangierino offers several menus catered to the varied tastes and requirements of larger parties and we can design private events suitable for any age group or celebration.
